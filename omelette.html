Basic Omelette

Description

A general omelette recipe that is great to be customized with added ingredients that you prefer.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 Tbsp butter
  • 1/4 cup finely graded cheese
  • 2 Tbsp Milk
  • (Optional) 1/3 cup cooked pork sausage

Steps

  1. Crack eggs into a bowl and stir with a fork to break the yoke.
  2. Mix in milk
  3. Heat up butter on medium high heat in a skillet on the stovetop. Melt it until butter is completely melted and slightly bubbling.
  4. Pour eggs into hot skillet, let sit for 1 minute while eggs cook.
  5. Immediately sprinkle cheese over eggs to melt while cooking.
  6. Once eggs have cooked on the bottom, take a spatulla and gently flip eggs halfway to form something resembling a quesadilla. Cook for another 30 seconds.
  7. Flip the omelette over to cook the other side for 30 seconds.
  8. Serve warm and eat immediately.
