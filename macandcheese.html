Macaroni and Cheese

Description

This is a basic recipe for delicious macaroni and cheese, a wonderful side dish for any southern meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 box of elbow macaroni
  • 1/2 cup Milk
  • 2 cups of Velveeta cheese, dice into small squares.
  • 1 Tbsp butter or olive oil

Steps

  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil on high temperature on a stovetop.
  2. Once water is boiling, dump the entire box of elbow macaroni into the boiling water. Boil for 10-12 minutes, or until noodles are soft and chewy."
  3. Once noodles have softened, drain water and mix in butter/oil, milk, and cheese. Stir until cheese is melted and mixed with milk.
  4. Serve warm with the rest of a delicious dinner.
